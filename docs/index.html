Features

Everything you need for protein design

From MCMC generation to 3D visualization — a complete autonomous pipeline.

Generation

MCMC Engine

Parallel tempering with configurable temperatures. Metropolis-Hastings acceptance. BLOSUM62 and ESM-2 guided proposals for biologically plausible mutations.

Scoring

Multi-Objective Energy

Binding affinity, folding stability, solubility, charge, and hydrophobicity — weighted and optimized simultaneously. Customizable per target.

Iteration

Autonomous Improvement

Each round uses the best candidate as the next seed. More MCMC steps and chains per round for progressively finer convergence.

Visualization

3D Structure Viewer

Interactive viewer with mutation highlighting. Load any PDB file and see designed mutations displayed on the protein backbone in real time.

Audit

Full Traceability

Every mutation proposed, every acceptance decision, every energy delta recorded. Downloadable JSON with complete run history and parameters.

Compliance

Research Use Only

Audit logging, dual-use screening, data retention policies. Designed for regulatory-aligned research workflows. Not a medical device.

About

Built by researchers, for researchers

Proteus was developed to bridge computational protein design and wet-lab validation.

Proteus is an autonomous protein design platform that uses Markov Chain Monte Carlo to generate novel peptide therapeutics. It was designed to reduce the in-silico protein design cycle from months to minutes, enabling researchers to explore more candidate space before committing to synthesis and wet-lab testing.

The system combines a from-scratch MCMC engine (Metropolis-Hastings with parallel tempering), multi-objective energy scoring (binding, stability, solubility), and real-time 3D structure visualization — all accessible through a conversational agent interface.

Proteus is benchmarked against AlphaFold structure predictions, published therapeutic binders from peer-reviewed literature, and FDA-approved drugs where available. Performance data is scraped in real time from public databases including AlphaFold DB, RCSB PDB, PubMed, and DrugBank.

The platform is designed for regulatory-aligned research workflows with full audit logging, dual-use screening, and data retention policies. All code is open source.
